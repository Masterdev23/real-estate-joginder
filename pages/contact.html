Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by filling out our contact form or calling us directly. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours.

What documents do I need?

Required documents typically include ID proof, income verification, and credit history. Contact us for a detailed list.

How long does the process take?

The entire process usually takes 2-4 weeks, depending on various factors. Our team will guide you through each step.

What are the payment options available?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, mortgage financing, and structured payment plans. Contact our financial advisors for details.

Can I rent before buying?

Yes, we offer rent-to-own options on selected properties. This allows you to experience the property before making a final purchase decision.

What are the maintenance charges?

Maintenance charges vary by property and include security, cleaning, and upkeep. Detailed charges will be provided during property viewing.
